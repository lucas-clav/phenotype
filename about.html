PHENOTYPE ABOUT

about the project

Phenotype is a series of interactive sketches that explores typography through the rhythms and movements of the human body.

This project utilizes Laban Movement Analysis (LMA), derived from Rudolf Laban’s system for analyzing movement, as the primary input framework. Through body and hand tracking, the project maps the eight LMA Efforts directly onto the parameters of digital type. Users are invited to perform each LMA Effort to transcribe their physical rhythm into kinetic typography, creating an anthropomorphic type specimen.
